OUR GOALS

  • Want to create amity & brotherhood among all former students of Haripur thana
  • Try to elavated the glory & tradition of Haripur
  • Try to ensure the highest right of Haripur
  • Try to support all former & present students for employment of Hripur
  • At any necessary or required to support the former and current students maximum
  • Current and former students of the records of all members of the organization.
  • FAH records of all members of the organization's current and former students to be prepared
  • The maximum ability of the organization to participate in the education, social development and cultural activities.
  • We will try to maintain close relation among all students, officers & employees.
  • We welcome talented students, providing scholarships to poor but talented students and providing educational materials to be supplied and special training facilities.
  • We try to arragne competitive programs in a variety of different types of educational institutions and seminars.
  • We try to provide free part-time education in various educational initiatives.
  • To facilitate program activities are conducted jointly with other organizations. All public and private organizations to establish a relationship with the developmental program.
